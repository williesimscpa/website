Who We Are

Established in 1999, Willie B. Sims, Jr., CPA, PLLC is a long-time standing CPA firm servicing the Hattiesburg community and surrounding southeast MS region. Our firm is committed to upholding our reputation of dedicated service to our clients while continuing to maintain our objective of providing advisory services to individuals, businesses, non-profit and government organizations. We maintain a quality control program to ensure that our work meets professional standards (we are our top priority!)

Professional Credentials
  • Certified Public Accountant (CPA)
  • Bachelor's Degree in Accounting
  • Member, American Institute of CPAs
  • Member, Mississippi Society of CPAs
  • Established 1999
